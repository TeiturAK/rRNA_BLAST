# About why maximum sensitivity was not used
After 5 days running time it did not seem like the max sensitivity was making that big of a difference in the number of sequences it found. 
The non-sensitive mode found ~19000.
Sensitive mode found 11800 after 5 days.
11500 of the 11800 were found in the less sensitive search which I tested with:
bedtools intersect -u -a tRNAscan-SE_28-June_snowy_2-max/results.gff -b tRNAscan-SE_28-June_snowy_2/results.gff | wc -l
